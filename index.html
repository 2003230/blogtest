US and Canada hit by major winter storm

A major winter storm has hit parts of the United States and Canada, causing widespread power outages and travel disruption.

Snow, ice and freezing temperatures have affected large areas, with states of emergency declared in several regions.

In Canada, more than 200,000 people were without electricity, while in the US states of Texas and Louisiana, millions were left in the dark.

The storm has been blamed for several deaths, including a person hit by a snowplough in New York.
